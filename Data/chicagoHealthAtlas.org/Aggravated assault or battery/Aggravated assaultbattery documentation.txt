Aggravated assault/battery

crimes

Aggravated assault and aggravated battery crimes. Aggravated assault is an unlawful attack by one person upon another, wherein the offender displays a weapon in a threatening manner. Aggravated battery is the physical attack itself, wherein the offender uses a weapon or the victim suffers obvious severe or aggravated bodily injury involving apparent broken bones, loss of teeth, possible internal injury, severe laceration, or loss of consciousness.
